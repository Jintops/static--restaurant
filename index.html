About Us

WHY CHOOSE US?

Best Food In The Country

Here at Tasty Restaurant,we are committed to bringing you the best in cuisine, from both India and abroad that is why we source all our ingredients locally, where possible using only the best fresh produce, we also use finest imported food items like Italian pasta, extra virgin olive oil, and cheeses. All our bread and dishes are made by our master baker in the oven, look out of our selection of dishes and pastries that are freshly made each day or ask our waiters for the bake of the day. For that perfect wakeup call try one of our espresso coffee's sourced directly from the south Indian coffee heartlands of the Coorg and Malabar, made on our stovetop Italian espresso machine its also a perfect afternoon accompaniment to our selection of dishes..
